Nikki Ranking Helper

Little helper to help you figure out which stages to rank on next.

Columns

  • New Items: Number of new items compared to your last saved wardrobe on this stage.
  • Max Base Score: Maximum base score according to NI.
  • Own Score: Your base score for this stage.
  • Difference: Difference between the max base score and your score (lower is better!).

Last Updated

  • When we last pulled the data from NI. To avoid overwhelming her servers, this tool only automatically refreshes every 5 days.
